Pizza

Description

This is a classic pepperoni pizza recipe with tastful mozzerella and creamy tomato sauce and flavorful pepperoni ontop of very fluffy dough for the crust that is guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 0.5lbs of Pepperoni
  • 1 m^3 of mozzerella
  • 1 can of premade infinity dough
  • 1 light speed oven
  • Optional* 1 sibling to make this for you so you can just eat and not work

Steps

  1. preheat your oven to 4000 kelvin
  2. Take your infinity dought and kneed it until it is the size of your desired pizza
  3. Second spread the tomato sauce across your pizza evenly
  4. Third spread your cheese and pepperoni to your desired amounts ontop of the sauce
  5. Place your pizza in the lightspped oven and let it cook for 1 eternity
  6. Take out your pizza and let it cool for 30 seconds and then ENJOY!
